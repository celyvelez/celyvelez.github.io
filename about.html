Awards

Longlist | Digital Media Award Apr 2023
Issued by: One World Media

Juego de Votos

Game of Votes (GoV) is the universe built by Cuestión Pública to help Colombians decide how to vote in the 2022 legislative and presidential elections. We created an innovative web application —inspired by the HBO television series "Game of Thrones"—which investigates the connections between wealthy families and political campaigns. We reveal political clans' money trail and interests ahead of elections, aimed to captivate first-time voters. GoV exposes the candidates of these political Houses that seek a seat in Congress and are running for office. CP cross-referenced, analyzed, and visualized information from at least 22 databases in Colombia and abroad.

¿Así cuidamos a nuestros primates? Una historia de conservación y tráfico ilegal

This is the story of Matías, a woolly monkey who was rescued from illegal trafficking and, after years of rehabilitation, was fortunate enough to be reintegrated into his natural habitat in Vaupés, a department in southeastern Colombia. But not all primates are so lucky.

Nominees | Best Webdoc LATAM Mar 2022
Issued by: CuscoWebFest

Siete horas de angustia en La Modelo

On 21 March 2020, hundreds of inmates of La Modelo prison in Bogotá rioted, demanding attention to the Covid-19 pandemic. The reaction of the guards, supported by the police and the army, left 24 inmates dead and almost a hundred injured, including 11 guards. We reconstruct what happened that night using open-source images, visual analysis, and the testimonies of prisoners and guards.

Shortlist | Sigma Award Mar 2022
Issued by: Sigma

Sabemos lo que hiciste la legislatura pasada

This is the 1st web application revealing the assets, business & network of more than 75 Congress members, chosen by the audience on two open surveys. We exposed their companies, family businesses, state contracts, networks, and private interests to improve citizens' decision-making. Indi$cregram: the indiscreet Instagram of power in Colombia. Inspired by the social network, this app informs citizens about who they voted for in Congress before the 2022 elections. This tool is based on a methodology to collect, analyze and cross information from 40 public information sources in Colombia and abroad to consolidate around 3500 entities.
